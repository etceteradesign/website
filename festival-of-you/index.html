Festival Of You

Urban Outfitters have been continually criticised for their negative influence on the self esteem and confidence in young people, especially young girls. 'Festival of You' is a concept designed to alter the brands damaged reputation. The event offers customers a 2 day celebration of themselves and a chance to improve their confidence through a series of inspirational talks and workshops.
